Employment

The Renegade Girls Tinkering Club offers fun and community for children to explore the world of tinkering and making. Children get hands-on with their peers to see how things work. Tinkering, a process involving hands-on discovery, and trial and error toward a goal, helps girls develop grit and gives a solid foundation for STEM skills. Founded by mothers in STEM careers working to change the vast gender imbalance in their fields, the Renegade Girls Tinkering Club inspires girls to keep their love of scientific exploration.

Summer Camp Directory

We are looking for a Camp Director for our Summer Camps to supervise our camp counselors and make operations happen seamlessly. What do Camp Directors do? The most important out of all Camp Director duties is to ensure camp staff are working effectively, and that all campers are safe at all times. The job duties of a Camp Director include supervising camp staff, scheduling substitutes when a staffer is unavailable, ensuring that the staff is operating effectively, that the campers are happy, and that the site is cleaned up each day. Being good at resolving interpersonal conflicts and able to build good relationships with adults, teens, and children are two essential traits of a Camp Director. You need to have knowledge around helping children to resolve differences and how to motivate staff. If you’re experienced teaching elementary school children and have good relationships with your coworkers, we’d like to meet you.

Lead Camp Counselor

We are looking for one more Lead Camp Counselor for our Summer Camps to supervise young campers and make sure they learn and have fun. What do Camp Counselors do? The most important out of all Camp Counselor duties is to ensure campers are safe at all times. You’ll be responsible for overseeing their activities and ensuring they don’t get lost or hurt. The job duties of a Camp Counselor include supervising summer camp games, educating children, and setting up equipment. Being reliable and able to build good relationships with children are two essential traits of a Camp Counselor. You need to have knowledge around helping children to resolve differences and to listen when they get excited. If you’re patient, energetic but strict when needed, we’d like to meet you. Email your resume, summer availability, and why you're interested in the job. We don't respond to incomplete applications.

Assistant Counselor

We are looking for a few great high school students to work as Assistant/Junior Camp Counselors for our Summer Camps. What do Assistant Camp Counselors do? Assistants operate under the supervision of a Lead Counselor, but also have an important role to play. The most important out of all Camp Counselor duties is to ensure campers are safe at all times. You’ll help to oversee their activities and ensure they don’t get lost or hurt. The job duties of an Assistant Camp Counselor include supervising summer camp games and setting up equipment. Being reliable and able to build good relationships with children are two essential traits of an Assistant Camp Counselor. If you’re patient, energetic, and enjoy working with kids, we’d like to meet you. Email your resume, summer availability, and why you're interested in the job. We don't respond to incomplete applications, or to parents emailing for their teens.
