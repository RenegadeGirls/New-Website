Camp costs should not be a barrier for kids to attend a high quality, meaningful summer program. Please help us to provide free camp to kids who otherwise could not afford it. For the past three years, we worked with Summer Together and private donors to fund free camp for low income kids. Summer Together covers the lowest income families, but the FHA poverty level in San Francisco is now $148,000 for a family of four, due to the high cost of living, making summer camp out of reach for what would be a comfortable living in a different city. All donations are tax deductible; Renegade Girls is a 501(c)(3) organization. Renegade Girls is registered with many directed donation funds and donation matching sites. Your donation, whether $5000, $500, $50, or $5, helps provide free camp.
